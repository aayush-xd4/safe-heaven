Empowering Women in Times of Crisis

Safe Heaven provides comprehensive support, resources, and empowerment to ensure women's safety, well-being, and resilience during disasters and challenging times. Our platform offers a range of services designed to address the unique needs of women in crisis situations.

Get Started

Real-Time Disaster Response Hub

Stay informed and connected during emergencies with our real-time disaster response hub. Access critical information, locate safe shelters, and connect with emergency services. Our platform provides up-to-date alerts, interactive maps, and community support networks to ensure you have the resources you need when it matters most.

Access Hub

Menstrual Health Tracker

Take control of your menstrual health with our comprehensive tracking tool. Monitor your cycle, symptoms, and overall well-being, especially during crisis situations. Our tracker provides personalized insights, hygiene tips, and access to essential resources tailored to your needs during emergencies.

Start Tracking

IPV Support Chatbot

Get immediate, confidential support and resources for intimate partner violence. Our AI-powered chatbot provides a safe space to discuss your concerns, access emergency services, and connect with professional support. Available 24/7, our chatbot offers guidance, safety planning, and links to local resources to help you navigate challenging situations.

Chat Now

Pregnancy and Motherhood Forum

Connect with other mothers and access vital information for maternal health during disasters. Our forum provides a supportive community where you can share experiences, ask questions, and receive expert advice on prenatal care, childbirth, and infant care in challenging circumstances. Access resources on emergency birthing procedures, infant nutrition, and postpartum care during crises.

Join Forum

Volunteer Matching Platform

Make a difference by volunteering your skills and time to support women in need. Our platform connects passionate individuals with opportunities to assist in disaster relief, provide emotional support, or offer professional services. Whether you're a medical professional, counselor, or simply want to lend a helping hand, join our network of volunteers dedicated to empowering women during crises.

Volunteer Now

Crowdsourced Funding for Women

Support women in crisis through our transparent and efficient crowdfunding platform. Contribute to campaigns that provide emergency relief, medical assistance, or long-term recovery support for women affected by disasters. Our platform ensures that your donations reach those in need quickly and effectively, making a real impact in the lives of women facing challenging circumstances.

Donate or Fundraise

Personalized Emergency Preparation Plans

Create a custom emergency plan tailored to your unique needs and circumstances. Our interactive tool guides you through the process of developing a comprehensive preparedness strategy, including evacuation routes, emergency contacts, and essential supplies. Receive personalized recommendations based on your location, family situation, and specific vulnerabilities to ensure you're ready for any crisis.

AI Chatbot Assistance

Get immediate support and answers to your questions with our AI-powered chatbot. Available 24/7, our chatbot can provide information on emergency procedures, health concerns, and available resources. It's your go-to assistant for quick, reliable information during times of crisis.

Voice Assistant

Use voice commands for hands-free access to emergency features and information. Our voice assistant can help you navigate the platform, access critical information, and even call for help when you're unable to use your hands. It's designed to be your reliable companion in any emergency situation.
